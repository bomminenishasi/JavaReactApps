## 1. Getting Started

Open your browser and navigate to:

```
http://localhost:3000
```

You land on the **Home Page**. No login is required to browse products or add items to your cart.

---

## 2. Browsing Products

### Home Page

The home page shows:
- A hero banner with a call-to-action button
- Category tiles (Electronics, Clothing, Grocery, Home & Garden, Sports & Outdoors, Toys & Games, Beauty, Automotive)
- Featured / trending products

### Browsing by Category

Click any **category tile** on the home page or use the top navigation bar to filter products by category.

### Product List Page

| Feature | How to use |
|---|---|
| **Filter by category** | Click a category tile or nav link |
| **Search** | Type in the search bar at the top |
| **Sort** | Use the sort dropdown (Price: Low-High, High-Low, Newest) |
| **Pagination** | Click page numbers at the bottom |

### Product Detail Page

Click any product card to open its detail page. You will see:
- Product images
- Full description
- Price and stock status
- **Add to Cart** button

## 3. Shopping Cart

### Adding Items (No Account Required)

You can add items to your cart **without signing in**. Items are saved in your browser and persist across page refreshes.

- Click **Add to Cart** on any product card or product detail page.
- A toast notification confirms the item was added.
- The cart icon in the header shows the current item count.

### Viewing the Cart

Click the cart icon (top right) to open your cart. You can:
- See all items with quantities and prices
- Increase or decrease quantity using **+** / **−** buttons
- Remove an item with the **Remove** button
- See the **subtotal**, **estimated tax**, and **order total**

### Guest Cart

If you are not signed in, your cart is saved locally in your browser. When you sign in or create an account, your guest cart items are automatically merged into your account cart.

---

## 4. Creating an Account

1. Click **Sign In** in the top-right header, then click **Create an account** at the bottom of the login form.  
   — OR — click **Register** directly from any page.

2. Fill in:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Email** — used to log in
   - **Phone** *(optional)*
   - **Password** — minimum 8 characters

3. Click **Create Account**.

4. If you had guest cart items, they are automatically saved to your new account.

---

## 5. Signing In

1. Click **Sign In** in the top-right header.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
3. Click **Sign In**.

If you had guest cart items, they will be merged into your account cart automatically.

> **Forgot your password?** Contact support via the Help Center link in the footer.

---

## 6. Checkout

You must be **signed in** to complete a purchase.

### Steps

1. Open your **cart** and click **Proceed to Checkout** (or **Sign In to Checkout** if not signed in yet).
2. **Shipping Address** — enter your delivery address.
3. **Payment Details** — enter your card information.
4. Review your **Order Summary** — items, shipping, tax, total.
5. Click **Place Order**.

You will receive an order confirmation on screen with your **Order ID**.

---

## 7. Orders & Order History

### Viewing Orders

1. Sign in to your account.
2. Click your name or the user icon in the top-right header.
3. Select **My Orders** from the dropdown.

You will see a list of all past orders with:
- Order ID and date
- Order status (Processing, Shipped, Delivered, Cancelled)
- Total amount

### Order Detail

Click any order to view its full details:
- Itemised product list
- Shipping address
- Payment method used
- Current order status and tracking info

## 8. Your Profile

1. Sign in and click your name in the top-right header.
2. Select **Profile** or **Account Settings**.

You can update:
- First/Last name

## 9. FAQ

**Q: Do I need an account to browse products?**  
A: No. All products are visible to everyone without an account.

**Q: Do I need an account to add items to my cart?**  
A: No. You can add items as a guest. They are saved in your browser until you check out or clear your browser data.

**Q: What happens to my cart when I log in?**  
A: Your guest cart items are automatically merged with your account cart.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex).

**Q: How do I track my order?**  
A: Go to My Orders and click on the relevant order. The status is updated as your order progresses.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**  
A: Orders can be cancelled while in **Processing** status. Open the order detail and click **Cancel Order**. Once shipped, cancellations are not possible.

**Q: What is the return policy?**  
A: Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Visit the [Returns & Refunds](/info/returns) page in the footer for full details.

**Q: How do I contact support?**  
A: Visit the [Help Center](/info/help-center) link in the footer.
